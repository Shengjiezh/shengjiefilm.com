About Shengjie

I founded Shengjie Film, LLC to honor the pace of film: deliberate, patient, and beautifully imperfect. From Sea Ranch getaways with my wife to quiet streets in Copenhagen, I chase honest light and the small details that make a day unforgettable.

Shoots available across the Bay Area and beyond — couples, families, lifestyle, and editorial. Commercial inquiries welcome.
